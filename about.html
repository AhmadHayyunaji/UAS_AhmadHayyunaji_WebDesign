BIOGRAPHIES

Lovejoy are an indie rock band formed in Brighton, England. The band consists of William Gold (better known as Wilbur Soot) as lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist, Joe Goldsmith as lead guitarist, Mark Boardman as drummer, and Ash Kabosu as bassist, with all four also sharing in songwriting.[4] Their debut EP, Are You Alright?, was released on 8 May 2021. Their second EP, Pebble Brain, came out on 14 October 2021. The band's third EP, Wake Up & It's Over, was released on 12 May 2023.

The band independently release their music under their own label, Anvil Cat Records, distributing with AWAL.
